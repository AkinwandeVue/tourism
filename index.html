GLOBAL INITIATIVE FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF YOUTH TOURISM (YTI)

A full incorporated Non-Governmental Organization is espousing the concept of Youth Tourism Development and Promotion, as a platform for a sustainable tourism master plan initiative. Our organization’s initiative, was incorporated in June 2009, and has put forward various successful programs and activities like; the Pilot Practical Seminar on the Re-Orientation of Youths on Tourism and Tourism Development, The Nigeria’s Youth Tourism Visual Arts Competitions and Exhibitions, The Nigeria’s Youth Tourism Local Food Display, The Nigeria’s Youth Tourism Traditional Music presentations, The Nigeria’s Youth Tourism Talent Show amongst others.

OUR VISION

To create a global initiative that will transform the Nigeria’s Tourism and Culture Sectors, into an attractive environment that is exclusively driven by youths to promote Creativity, Innovation, Inter-Personal Skills, and Teamwork, in our own way of building sustainable strategies to promote Nigeria’s Cultural History, through her Sights and Sound, in the near future..

Youth Tourism Conference on the Re-orientation of youths on Tourism
In furtherance of the pursuance of the objectives of the Nigeria’s Federal Ministry of Tourism, Culture and National Orientation, through its Government-Parastatals and Sector-Stakeholders to put the Nigeria’s Tourism and Culture secctors towards attaining the front burner of National discourse and beyond, our organization have; through the collective efforts of achieving objectives necessary to promote the National Development Plan in the Country, set our vision towards creating and transforming the culture and tourism sectors into an attractive environment that is exclusively driven by Youths to promote Creativity, Innovations, Inter-personal skills and team work. These we hope to achieve in our own way of building sustainable strategies to promote Nigeria’s Cultural History, through Nigeria’s Sights and Sounds, whilst serving as a platform to boost the socio-economic base of the country to actualize the core goal of Self-Reliance Continue
Re-orientation of youths on Tourism

However, Nigeria has in the recent time gained reasonable income through its unique environment, from both local and International tourism. It is therefore vital to recognize and support one of the world’s fastest growing tourism sector; The Youth Tourism Sector, which have, as it were, the capabilities of reforming Nigeria, onto Economic- Tourism-Bracket Train. It is for this purpose that, “THE GLOBAL INITIATIVE FOR THE DEVELOPMENT OF YOUTH TOURISM, (YTI); a full incorporated Non-Governmental Organization is espousing the concept of Youth Tourism Development and Promotion, as a platform for a sustainable tourism master plan initiative.

Additionally, in our objectives of serving as the foundation through which the “evangelism” of youth tourism development is harnessed to achieving sustainable global business tourism, create database and research platform that would evaluate future tourism action plans, also, redirect the minds of Individuals, Private Sector Stakeholders, Private establishments, International agencies, and Governments, towards investing in tourism, especially in the Youth Tourism Sector.

Hence, in order to activate commercialization platforms of this almost untapped source of national income, and perhaps, through the establishment of Nigeria’s Youth Tourism Clubs; integrate these mobilized youth within localized skills acquisition activities as tour-guides, practitioners of Arts and Crafts,etc and thus build platforms for self- reliance through self-employment ,with the attendant effect on our presently high unemployment figures, we welcome you to our site and wish you are inspired by our actions and plans towards building a Dream of Hope and Future for our energetic youths. Do have a happy view.
